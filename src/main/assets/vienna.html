VIENNA

Vienna is the national capital and also the cultural, economic, and political centre of Austria.

Vienna is Austria's most populous city, with about 2 million inhabitants (2.6 million within the metropolitan area,[10] nearly one third of the country's population) , It is the 6th-largest city by population within city limits in the European Union.

Until the beginning of the 20th century, Vienna was the largest German-speaking city in the world, and before the splitting of the Austro-Hungarian Empire in World War I, the city had 2 million inhabitants. Today, it is the second-largest German-speaking city after Berlin.
